How this works

1. Download the phantom wallet extension to your browser. This will serve as your digital wallet.

3. Click on the "join the auction" button above and choose your favorite SOLinion.

What is this about?

SOLinions is a NFT Project on the Solana blockchain

We have created 10.000 individual SOLinions with far more than 100 different traits making each and every SOLinion unique. However, with the increasing amount of NFT projects in the Solana ecosystem, we will not mint all of them at once, but rather start with 9 SOLinions in the first edition. Starting price will 0.1 SOL, just covering our minting and auction costs and is therefore much lower than in your common NFT mints. We want to give everyone the chance to own a SOLinion.

The first 9 SOLinions are available for auction at our Holaplex website

By owning one of the first 9 SOLinions, you will be the core crew of this project. For that you will be rewarded of course. You will be one of the creators of the second edition and we will share 80% of 2nd edition royalties with you. What does that mean? Each time a 2nd edition SOLinion is sold on the secondary market, 10% of the sale price will be kept as royalties. Out of these royalties, 80% will be split up between 1st generatio SOLinions buyers. You will be hard-coded in the respective metadata so that each time a 2nd generation SOLinion is sold, you will automatically receive your share in your wallet - forever.

Be part of the SOLinions and join us in the adventure!

Let's get in this together. We want to give you as early supporters the chance, to be part of SOLinions and to profit from the further advancement of the project.

Some SOLinions are rarer than others.

Rare items are spread equally in all editions. There might already a pretty rare one in edition 1. After edition 2 or 3, we will publish a rarity chart to our homepage to add some extra fun to the auctions.

Roadmap

Monthly new editions in increasing size

10.000 unique SOLinions have been created. An increasing amount will be dropped every month on our Holaplex store and will shortly after be listed on secondary market.

Owners of the previous edition receive 80% of royalties - hard coded and automatically

We give you a few days to trade your SOLinions on secondary market, if you wish to do so. On 1st of every month at 2pm EST, owners of previous edition SOLinions will have their wallets hard-coded into the metadata of the next edition and receive 80% of royalties forever. Royalties will be 10% which means, that every time a SOLinion is sold on the secondary market, 10% of the price is split up and transferred to the wallets automatically.

5% of SOLinions in each edition are randomly given to current SOLinion holders

Easy - we want to support the community. Don't flip your SOLinions. The longer you hold, the higher your chances of winning another SOLinion. There is value in holding your SOLinion, it won't be worthless after the subsequent edition.
