What is this about?

SOLinions is a NFT Project on the Solana blockchain

Each SOLinion is working on bringing benefit to the Solana ecosystem. By adopting one, you can be part of the adventure! Please treat your SOLinion well, otherwise they can get quite nasty, we can tell you that!

The first 10 SOLinions will be available for auction at our Holaplex website.

Get in there and join the auction - By owning one of the first 10 SOLinions, you will be the core supporters of this project - and for that you will be rewarded of course. Decide how many SOLinons will be auctionned next and decide on starting price. Of course, you will be hard-coded in the .json files, so you you can profit from every SOLinion that is sold or bought in the future - 5% of royalties will go to your wallet.

Be part of the SOLinions and join us in the adventure!

Let's get in this together. We want to give you as early supporters the chance, to be part of SOLinions, decide on the further advances of the project and earn your share in secondary market fees. We want this to be a true community project. And we want everyone to be able to participate, therefore the starting price in the auction will way less than your average minting price for big NFT drops.
