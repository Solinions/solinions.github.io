How this works

1. Download the phantom wallet extension to your browser. This will serve as your digital wallet.

2. Fund your wallet. You can use Binance or Coinbase or any other Crypto exchange provider for that.

3. Click on the "join the auction" button above and choose your favorite SOLinion.

What is this about?

SOLinions is a NFT Project on the Solana blockchain

We have created 5.000 individual SOLinions with far more than 100 different traits making each and every SOLinion unique. However, with the increasing amount of NFT projects in the Solana ecosystem, we will not mint all of them at once, but rather start with 9 SOLinions in Gen0, 12 SOLinions in Gen1 and 10 SOLinions in Gen2. Starting price will 0.1 SOL, just covering our minting and auction costs and is therefore much lower than in your common NFT mints. We want to give everyone the chance to own a SOLinion.

The first 33 SOLinions are available for auction at our Holaplex website and on digitaleyes

By owning one of the first SOLinions, you will be the core crew of this project. For that you will be rewarded of course. You will be one of the creators of the subsequent generation and we will share 80% of royalties with you. What does that mean? Each time a Gen1 SOLinion is sold on the secondary market, 10% of the sale price will be kept as royalties. Out of these royalties, 80% will be split up between original Gen0 SOLinions buyers. The Gen0 wallet will be hard-coded in the respective metadata so that each time a Gen1 SOLinion is sold, winners of the auction for Gen0 will automatically receive their share in their own wallet. And so it continues for the following Generations. Win the auction for Gen1 and recieve secondary market royalties for Gen2. As with growing community prices will rise, you should get in early!

Be part of the SOLinions and join us in the adventure!

Let's get in this together. We want to give you as early supporters the chance, to be part of SOLinions and to profit from the further advancement of the project.

Some SOLinions are rarer than others.

Rare items are spread equally in all generations. There might already a pretty rare one in Gen0. After Gen 2 or 3, we will publish a rarity chart to our homepage to add some extra fun to the auctions.

Roadmap

Monthly new generations in increasing size

5.000 unique SOLinions have been created. An increasing amount will be dropped every month on our Holaplex store and will shortly after be listed on secondary market.

Owners of the previous generation receive 80% of royalties - hard coded

Winners of the auction of the previous generation SOLinions will automatically receive 80% of royalties for every sale of a next generation SOLinion on the secondary market.

5% of SOLinions in each generation are randomly given to current SOLinion holders

Easy - we want to support the community. Don't flip your SOLinions. The longer you hold, the higher your chances of winning another SOLinion. There is value in holding your SOLinion, it won't be worthless after the subsequent generation.
